About Me

My name is Ummu Saiyidatul Balqis.

I'm 20 years old. I was born on February 19, 2004 at Hospital Sultanah Nur Zahirah Kuala Terengganu.

I grew up in the beautiful city of Kuala Terengganu with my family. I have two siblings and I'm the youngest one.

I'm continuing my studies at UiTM Kedah in the course Library Informatics which consists of five semesters.

Keropok Lekoris my go-to grub, and nothing hits the spot like ice-cold chocolate. As for kicking back and soaking up good vibes, always find me at the beach. It's my sanctuary, my escape from the hustle and bustle of uni life.
